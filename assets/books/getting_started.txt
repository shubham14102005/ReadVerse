Welcome to ReadVerse!
==================

ReadVerse is your personal digital library for PDF and EPUB books.

Getting Started:
1. Add books by tapping the "+" button (when available)
2. Tap on any book to start reading
3. Use the heart icon to mark books as favorites
4. Track your reading progress automatically

Features:
- Beautiful card-based book display
- Reading progress tracking
- Favorite books management
- Dark and light themes
- Cloud sync with Firebase

Enjoy your reading journey with ReadVerse!
